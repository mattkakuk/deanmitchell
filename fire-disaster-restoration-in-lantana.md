## **Why Fire Damage Is More Than Just Burn Marks**

Fire damage is like an iceberg—what you see is only a small part of the
problem. Flames are obvious, but the hidden damage often causes the biggest
headaches later.

### **Smoke Travels Like a Sneaky Fog**

Smoke moves through vents, hallways, attic spaces, and tiny gaps you didn’t
even know existed. Even if the fire was contained to one room, smoke can coat
the entire home.

### **Soot Is Small but Aggressive**

Soot particles are microscopic, acidic, and cling to surfaces like static
electricity. Leave it too long, and it can stain walls, corrode metal, and
permanently damage finishes.

### **Water Damage Often Follows Fire Damage**

Most fires are put out with water—meaning you can end up with soaked floors,
wet insulation, and elevated humidity. In Florida, that’s a mold risk waiting
to happen.
